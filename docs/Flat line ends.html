When Flat line ends is on, the pen is a square. With it off, the pen is a circle.

There are a few ways to turn Flat line ends on.

  1. Click on the setting button, the click on Flat line ends. Just click Flat line ends again to turn it off.
  1. You can also turn flat line ends on and off programmatically by using the set [flat line ends V] to <> block
