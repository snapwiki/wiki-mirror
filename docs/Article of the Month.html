Topic of the Month is a monthly event where Snappers are encouraged to make projects on a specific topic. It is known as TOTM for short.[1] All projects made as part of this event can be added to that month's TOTM studio by any Snapper. The TOTM studio is featured on the Snap! homepage for the duration of the month.

History

The very first topic of the month was "Friends". It was announced on November 4th, 2020 by bromagosa. He said, "Today, we're launching a new Topic of the Month initiative where's we'll encourage you to make projects around a particular topic and submit them to a shared collection that will be featured in the Snap! front page for the whole month." Since then, monthly TOTMs have been announced in the "Blog" subcategory of the forums every month.
