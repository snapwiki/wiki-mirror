Spam is something that is posted irreverently and unneeded in the situation it was posted. Examples include making duplicate topics, advertising oneself on others' topics, posting nonsense or gibberish, typing random keys on the keyboard, or posting off-topic replies to topics on the forum.

Spammers

A spammer is a user who spams constantly. They are people who are willing to misuse the site for spamming and advertising. Even though there are human spammers, there are also spambots (see section below).

Spambots

Spambots are programs written by spammers to create accounts for the purpose of spamming on websites automatically. Spambots are slowed down by certain features implemented to help stop them such as restriction on forum posts for new users.[1]
