Some things look the same and work the same, some things look the same and work a bit differently and of course, there are a lot of new features that Scratch simply does not have.

You can simply add a when @greenFlag clicked ::control, a forever loop, a move (10) steps::motion block, and a turn right (15) degrees::motion block. The result should be like this:
when @greenFlag clicked	::control
forever {
move (10) steps::motion
turn right (15) degrees::motion
}::control cap
and when you press the Green Flag, your sprite will move around in a circle just like Scratch.

However you might have noticed a yellow pause in-between the Flag and the stop button. Press it and your script will pause - pressing it again - it will resume from where it left off.
