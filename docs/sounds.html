This page is about sound effects. For the block category, see Sound
Sounds are assets you can add to sprites and the stage to play sounds effects, music, and voices. Plenty of blocks, primarily the Sound blocks, uses these sounds.

There are 4 ways to add your own sound effects.

  • Record one.
  • Drag & drop an audio file.
  • Add one from the media library.
  • Import audio via file > import


Snap! does not have a sound editor, but you can programatically get the samples of a sound and do computations on them to change the sound.
