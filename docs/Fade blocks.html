Fade blocks is a setting that fades the backgrounds of blocks and makes them look more like pieces of text attached together than blocks. It also removes the texture of the scripting area and darkens the sprite list.

  • 50% or more transparency will change the text to black (if in flat design) and after that the text will gradually become the category color.
  • 76% or more transparency will turn the input text color to white (if not in flat design).
