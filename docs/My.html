my () is a reporter block and a sensing block. It is used to extract various attributes of a sprite, for example, the sprite's sounds, their neighboring sprites and their name.

(my [ v]::sensing)

Options

  • neighbors: Reports sprites the sprite is touching
  • self: Reports the sprite itself
  • other sprites: Reports all the sprites excluding itself.
  • clones
  • other clones
  • parts
  • anchor
  • stage: Reports the stage.
  • children
  • parent
  • temporary?: Reports whether the sprite is temporary and eliminated when the [[Stop Sign] is clicked. Clones create programmatically are temporary.
  • name
  • scripts
  • solutions
  • costume: Reports the current costume of the sprite.
  • costumes: Reports all the costumes the sprite have (excluding Turtle)
  • sounds: Reports all the sounds the sprite have
  • blocks: Reports all the blocks available in the palette for this sprite (custom blocks are placed above primitives)
  • categories: Reports the names of the available categories (including Lists and Other)
