() of () is a reporter block and an Operators block. It reports the result of some math functions.

([sqrt V] of (10))

Options

  • abs: The absolute value or magnitude. Negative numbers get converted to positive, but positive numbers remain positive.
  • neg: Negates the value. Positive numbers are converted into negative and vice versa.
  • sign: Signum. Positive values report 1, negative reports -1, and zero reports 0.
  • ceiling: Rounds decimals up. For example, 3.6 is rounded up to 4.
  • floor: Rounds decimals down. For example, 3.6 is rounded down to 3.
  • sqrt: Square root.
  • sin: Sine. Degrees are used.
  • cos: Cosine.
  • tan: Tangent.
  • asin
  • acos
  • atan
  • ln: Natural logarithm, which is the logarithm of e (Euler's number).
  • log: Decimal logarithm, which is the logarithm of 10.
  • lg: Binary logarithm, which is the logarithm of 2.
  • e^: Euler's number to the power of ().
  • 10^
  • 2^
  • id: Identity, which reports the input. Useful for creating deep copies of lists, whereas without it will be a shallow copy.
